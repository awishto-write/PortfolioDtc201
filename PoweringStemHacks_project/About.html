Biography

My name is Naomi Dion-Gokan, a third-year Computer Science student at Washington State University, Pullman. I am the program chair for the National Society of Black Engineers(NSBE) in my school.
Also, I am currently interning at Schweitzer Engineering Laboratories as a Software Engineering intern. I enjoyed web development
I got the chance to be an officer for WSU Association of Computing Machinery where the other four officers and I organized the Crimson Code Hackathon 2023 where more 200 students team up for a 24-hours coding competition.
In addition, I am also a member of Girls Who Code, Rewriting The Code and recently ColorStack. I enjoy reading, cooking and swimming occasionally.
